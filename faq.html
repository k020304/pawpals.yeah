Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt a pet from PawPals?

Simply visit the pet listings section, choose a pet, and follow the adoption process steps. You will need to fill out an adoption form and schedule a meeting with the pet.

Is there an adoption fee?

Yes, we have a small adoption fee to cover the costs of veterinary care and other expenses. The fee varies depending on the animal.

Can I foster a pet?

Yes, you can foster a pet! We encourage fostering as it helps prepare pets for adoption and gives them a loving environment.

What should I do if I want to help PawPals?

You can volunteer, donate, or even become a foster parent! Every little bit helps the animals in need.
